GUI for Triple-I

Introduction

In 2017, Triple-I's webpage appeared, focusing on displaying savings insurance information, providing people who insured savings insurance with a new place to collect information. With the expansion of the company, more and more functions are required, and the visual structure of the overall website has to be rectified.

Certain data and research are hidden due to confidentiality agreements

Color Pallete

Triple-I originally set color is blue. I expanded the color system and added a pink auxiliary color in it. It is to make the overall cold color more interesting and soft as purpose. At the same time, various web colors are added as the basis for future production.

Mascot

The Astros mascot is an inherent part of the site. My job is to figure out how to use it in various places on our web pages, especially the social networking sites. The approach taken was to simplify and add expressions to pair with ultra-simplified aliens.

Pages

THe re-design website appearence. The project will add new functions and follow the formulate style system and content, so that it can have standard visual specifications that can benefit the next maintainer.
