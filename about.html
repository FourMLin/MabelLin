About Mabel

I’m a UI/UX designer with over 5 years of experience, currently graduating from the Royal College of Art with an MA in Service Design. I have worked with both startups and large tech companies, focusing on delivering B2B and B2C products across FinTech, e-commerce, and IoT industries.

I’ve collaborated with many cross-functional teams, departments and companies. I specialise at transforming diverse media, documents and technical insights into my design process, seamlessly integrating them into prototypes for balance requirement and creativity. My toolkit includes Figma, Adobe Creative Suite, HTML/CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, and Vue.js to support my insight and availability.
