Research

Health effect of air pollution

In 2016, Taiwan's air pollution index seriously exceeded the WHO standard. Factory and automobile exhaust accounted for the majority of pollution sources. Studies have shown that more than 70% of people have allergies, and over 50% of them have respiratory allergies.

People are eager for fully functional and more effective devices to clean the air. Futurelab Company developed the N8 purifier robot that can eliminate odor and remove harmful gases such like PM2.5 or PM1.0. Then, it have mobile function as a special feature.

Indoor pollution

Integrate exsisting product

We propose using the existing N7 purifier as a sensor to monitor air quality, triggering the N8 to purify specific areas as needed. This gives N7 added value beyond single-point testing and increases its market exposure.

Define

How it works?

The air purifier can move, as if a butler decides which solution would be taken according to different situations. During the implementation, N8 will arrange the best patrol cleaning plan for customers' space based on the current air quality by itself. How to judge the situation? It is based on the data before and after at the same area to determine the cleaning strength of the machine. And receiving the fixed-area data of the N7 (the small air purifier) is to determine whether to perform an emergency cleaning.
