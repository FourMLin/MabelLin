Health effect of air pollution

In 2016, Taiwan's air pollution index seriously exceeded the WHO standard. Factory and automobile exhaust accounted for the majority of pollution sources. Studies have shown that more than 70% of people have allergies, and over 50% of them have respiratory allergies.

People are eager for fully functional and more effective devices to clean the air. Futurelab Company developed the N8 purifier robot that can eliminate odor and remove harmful gases such like PM2.5 or PM1.0. Then, it have mobile function as a special feature.

Air purifier key feature

Our team provided company that N8 allows to automatically patrol and track the air quality of each room, record and provide the air condition in user's home. In our ideal, it walks around the room like a housekeeper and reports the condition in house.
